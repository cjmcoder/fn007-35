# Rewards & Economy Policy

This document outlines the complete reward system, currency types, fees, and payout structures for the platform.

## Currency Types

### FC (FlockCoin)
- **Primary Gaming Currency**: Used for match entry fees and payouts
- **Withdrawable**: Yes, users can cash out FC winnings
- **Entry Limits**: 1 FC minimum, 2,000 FC maximum per match
- **Source**: Deposits, match winnings, leaderboard rewards

### Boost Credits
- **Promotional Currency**: Bonus credits for enhanced gameplay
- **Withdrawable**: No, promotional use only
- **Usage**: Match entries, tournament entries
- **Source**: Welcome bonuses, promotional campaigns, loyalty rewards

### FNC (FlockNetwork Coin)
- **Blockchain Token**: Platform's native cryptocurrency
- **Withdrawable**: Yes, to external wallets
- **Usage**: Premium features, governance, staking rewards
- **Source**: Special events, high-tier achievements, token sales

- **Platform Fee**: 15% of total collected entry fees
- **Calculation**: Entry Fee √ó Participants √ó 15%

## Match Tiers & Requirements

### Tier 1 (Basic Quick Match)
- **Entry Range**: 1-24 FC
- **Platform Fee**: 5%
- **Requirements**: None
- **Features**: Standard community matches
- **Stream Required**: No

### Tier 2 (Private Server Match)
- **Entry Range**: 25-99 FC
- **Platform Fee**: 5%
- **Requirements**: 25+ FC entry
- **Features**: Private server, enhanced monitoring
- **Stream Required**: Yes (with MatchID in title)

### VIP Server (High Stakes)
- **Entry Range**: 100+ FC
- **Platform Fee**: 5%
- **Requirements**: Rank 8+ or invite-only
- **Features**: Exclusive arena, premium support
- **Stream Required**: Yes (with VOD verification)

## Verification & Anti-Fraud

### Stream Verification Requirements
- **Tier 2 & VIP**: Live stream with MatchID in title
- **VOD Storage**: 30-day minimum retention
- **Compliance Check**: Automated and manual review
- **Penalties**: Match forfeiture, temporary restrictions

### Trust Score System
- **Green (High)**: 90%+ verification rate, no disputes
- **Yellow (Medium)**: 70-89% verification rate, minor disputes
- **Red (Low)**: <70% verification rate, multiple disputes
